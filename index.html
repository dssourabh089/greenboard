🌱 About Greenboard Foundation

Greenboard Foundation is a voluntary, non-profit organization (NGO) dedicated to building a greener, healthier, and more sustainable world. We firmly believe that environmental protection and humanitarian service are the core pillars of a progressive society.

×

🌱 Our Vision & Mission

Our mission is to restore ecological balance while standing firmly by people in times of emergency to serve humanity.

×

🛠️ Our Work & Initiatives

🌱 Tree Plantation Drives: To combat climate change and maintain ecological balance, we actively organize and execute large-scale tree plantation campaigns.

🩸 Blood Donation Camps: Guided by the belief that "blood saves lives," we regularly host voluntary blood donation camps to support patients in critical need.

🧹 Environmental Cleanliness: We conduct regular cleanliness drives to keep our surroundings waste-free, plastic-free, and pollution-free.

📢 Raising Public Awareness: We organize community programs and awareness campaigns to educate the public on environmental conservation, the importance of nature, and civic responsibilities.
